Vocaloids

Vocaloids are computer-produced voices used for singing.

Vocaloids were developed by the Yamaha Corporation.There are multiple vocaloids, each with a different voice. Some of these vocaloids include:

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Len and Rin Kagamine
  • Megurine Luka

Vocaloids are usually shown as anime-style characters. Here is one such picture. This is Luka.

Vocaloids are sold as applications. Anybody with enough money can download and use them. Here are a few examples of user-created Vocaloid songs:

  • The Disappearance Of Hatsune Miku
  • O Light
  • Electric Angel
