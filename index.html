Vocaloids

Vocaloids are computer-produced voices used for singing. They are rapidly gaining fame all over the world due to the terrific songs that feature them. You may have heard of Hatsune Miku before- she is a vocaloid, along with many others. She mayy be the most famous one, however. Vocaloids were developed by the Yamaha Corporation. There are multiple vocaloids, each with a different voice. I have a list of official vocaloids on my other page, there is a link to it at the bottom of this page. Some of these vocaloids include:

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Len and Rin Kagamine
  • Megurine Luka
  • Kaito

Vocaloids are usually shown as anime-style characters. Here is one such picture. This is Kaito.

Vocaloids are sold as applications. Anybody with enough money can download and use them. Here are a few examples of user-created Vocaloid songs:

  • The Disappearance Of Hatsune Miku
  • O Light
  • Electric Angel
