Vocaloids are computer-produced voices used for singing. They are rapidly gaining fame all over the world due to the terrific songs that feature them.
You may have heard of Hatsune Miku before- she is a vocaloid, along with many others. Vocaloids were developed by the Yamaha Corporation.

There are multiple vocaloids, each with a different voice. I have a link to a list of officially recognized vocaloids on my "Different Vocaloids" page. Some of these vocaloids include:

  • Hatsune Miku
  • Len and Rin Kagamine
  • Megurine Luka
  • Kaito

Vocaloids are usually shown as anime-style characters. Here is one such picture. This is Kaito.

Vocaloids are sold as applications. Anybody with enough money can download and use them.

There are thousand upon thousands of vocaloid songs that have been created by users.
Some of these users have gained internet fame through their songs.
Here are a few examples of user-created Vocaloid songs:

  • The Disappearance Of Hatsune Miku
  • O Light
