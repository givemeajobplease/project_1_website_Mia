Here are quite a few officially recognized Vocaloids.
Some of these are produced by different companies, and there are many different languages used.

The original Vocaloids were created in Japan. They are the most commonly used.
This is the link to a website with an extensive list of vocaloids.

The most famous vocaloid is Hatsune Miku. Miku is pretty much the public face of vocaloids, you can find numerous articles in famous magazines about vocaloids with her picture being the main feature.
The second most famous vocaloids would probably be Len and Rin, a pair of blonde twins.
The third most famous would be Luka, then Kaito, and so on. Vocaloids aren't too well known to most of the population, but they are still pretty famous, with videos featuring them gaining millions of views.
